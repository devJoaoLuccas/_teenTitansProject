Teen Titans Animations

Introduction:

Welcome to the Animations section of our Teen Titans website! Here, you'll find information on all the animated adaptations of this beloved superhero team. From the classic 2003 animated series to the recent rebooted series, we have it all covered. The Teen Titans have been a fan-favorite among DC Comics enthusiasts for decades, and the animated adaptations have brought these characters to life in exciting ways. Whether you're a long-time fan or a newcomer to the world of Teen Titans, our Animations section has everything you need to know about these animated adaptations.

We'll delve into the storylines, characters, and animation styles of each series, providing you with a comprehensive guide to the Teen Titans animated universe. So, sit back, relax, and get ready to explore the exciting world of Teen Titans animations with us.

Timeline

Teen Titans (2003 - 2006)

This is the first animated series featuring the Teen Titans. It aired for five seasons and followed the adventures of Robin, Starfire, Raven, Beast Boy, and Cyborg as they fought various villains.

Teen Titans: Trouble in Tokyo (2006)

This is a standalone animated film that serves as the series finale of the 2003 Teen Titans series. The Titans travel to Tokyo to track down the notorious villain, Brushogun.

Teen Titans Go! (2013-present)

This is a more lighthearted animated series that features the same characters as the 2003 series, but in a more comedic setting. The show has been running for several seasons and has spawned numerous spin-offs and specials.

Justice League vs. Teen Titans (2016)

The film follows the story of the Justice League as they clash with the Teen Titans after the latter group is sent to take down a dangerous supernatural threat. As the two teams struggle to work together, they soon discover that their foe is the demon Trigon, who seeks to take over the world through his daughter Raven. The film features a number of iconic DC characters, including Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, and the Flash, as well as the Teen Titans' Robin, Starfire, Beast Boy, Blue Beetle, and Raven.

Teen Titans: The Judas Contract (2017)

A direct-to-video animated film that serves as an adaptation of the classic Teen Titans storyline. The film follows the Teen Titans as they take on the villainous Deathstroke, who has a mole in the team.

Teen Titans Go! To the Movies (2018)

A feature-length animated film that serves as a spin-off of the Teen Titans Go! TV series. The Teen Titans try to get their own movie made in order to become real superheroes.

Teen Titans Go! vs. Teen Titans (2019)

Direct-to-video animated film that serves as a crossover between the original Teen Titans series and the Teen Titans Go! series. The two teams must work together to stop the villainous Trigon.
