Heroes

Teen Titans - Weast coast

The original team and founding members of the Teen Titans reside in the Titans Tower. They are tasked with the security of Jump City, California, and any major conflicts happening within or outside of the world, often addressing issues with as much efficiency and maturity as adult superheroes. Below is a list of the five members, with Silkye as their mascot.

  • Robin (Leader)
  • Starfire
  • Cyborg
  • Raven
  • Beast Boy
  • Terra (Previously)

East Titans

The second group of Teen Titans to be founded. They first appeared in "The East Coast Titans - Part 1", where they united to help defeat Brother Blood. The East Coast Titans are tasked with the security of Steel City, presumably located in the northeastern United States. They appear to be the backup team to defend Jump City when the original Teen Titans are out of town (for real).

Cyborg was the first and original leader of the East Coast Titans, but after Brother Blood's attack against the East Coast Titans, Cyborg defeated Blood and realized that his place was with the original Teen Titans. He named Bumblebee the leader of the East Coast Titans before returning to Jump City with Robin, Raven, Beast Boy, and Starfire. "You already have a leader, you just need to listen to her." Although they are best known for defending Steel City, they are well known in Jump City after the events of the episode "For Real".

  • Bee (Leader)
  • Speedy
  • Aqualad
  • Más y Menos
  • Cyborg (Previously)

North Titans

The third team of heroes to be formed. They are first seen in Teen Titans #55, and are located in a fairly mountainous and wooded area, presumably in the northern part of the United States or Canada. However, in some comics, they are located in Chicago, due to its high crime rate.

  • Red Star (Leader)
  • Argenta
  • Kole
  • Gnnark
